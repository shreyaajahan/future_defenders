Understanding Bullying

Bullying can happen in many places, like school, playgrounds, or even online. It involves someone repeatedly hurting or intimidating others. Standing against bullying is essential for creating safe and respectful environments for everyone.

How Can We Prevent Bullying?

There are many ways to help prevent bullying. This includes promoting kindness, speaking up if you see bullying happening, and reaching out for help when needed. If you or someone you know is being bullied, remember to talk to a trusted adult for support.
