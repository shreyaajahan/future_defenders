About Child Helplines

Child helplines are crucial services designed to provide support, guidance, and protection for children in need. They offer a safe space for children to seek help, discuss their concerns, and find solutions. These helplines are often accessible 24/7, ensuring that assistance is available whenever it's needed.

How Do Child Helplines Help?

Child helplines are staffed by trained professionals who can address a variety of issues such as bullying, abuse, mental health, and family problems. They offer counseling, advice, and, in cases of emergency, can coordinate with local authorities to ensure the child's safety.
