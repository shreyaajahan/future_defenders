Know Your Responsibilities

Understanding your responsibilities is crucial for maintaining a safe and respectful environment for yourself and others. Here are some key responsibilities every child should know!

1. Respect for Others

Always treat others with kindness and respect. Everyone deserves to be treated well, regardless of differences.

2. Following Rules

Adhering to rules in school and at home helps create a safe environment for everyone. Rules are meant to protect you and your peers.

3. Being Honest

Always tell the truth. Honesty builds trust, which is important in all your relationships.

4. Caring for Yourself and Others

Look after your own well-being and help others in need. Compassion goes a long way!

5. Being a Good Citizen

Participate in community activities and help make your environment better. Every little effort counts!

6. Seeking Help When Needed

It’s okay to ask for help when you’re unsure about something. Always seek assistance from trusted adults.

7. Responsibility in Learning

Take your education seriously. Participate actively in your learning process and strive to do your best!

8. Reporting Issues

If you see something wrong or unfair, report it to a trusted adult. Speaking up helps protect yourself and others.
