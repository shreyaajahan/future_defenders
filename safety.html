Child Safety

Empowering Kids to Stay Safe

Why Child Safety is Important

Child safety is about ensuring that children are protected from harm and that they have a safe environment to grow, learn, and play. Understanding safety measures helps kids know how to handle situations that could be dangerous.

How Can We Stay Safe?

Children can learn ways to stay safe in different places like home, school, and outdoors. Always remember to stay with a trusted adult, avoid talking to strangers, and know when to ask for help. Child safety tips can keep us safe in emergencies too!
