About Ram Jethmalani

Ram Jethmalani was one of India's most renowned and respected lawyers, known for his sharp legal acumen and unwavering courage. Throughout his career, he took on high-profile cases and fought for justice, even when it meant challenging the status quo. A fearless advocate for civil rights, Jethmalani also served as a Member of Parliament and held several significant roles in government, using his influence to promote legal reforms and uphold justice.
