What is the Right to Education?

The Right to Education is a fundamental human right that ensures every child has the opportunity to receive a quality education. It is a key to individual empowerment and societal growth.

Importance of the Right to Education

  • Helps eradicate poverty and inequality.
  • Promotes individual empowerment and social development.
  • Strengthens economic and societal progress.
  • Encourages tolerance and global citizenship.

Laws Supporting Right to Education

In many countries, the Right to Education is protected under constitutional or statutory law. For example, in India, the Right to Education Act ensures free and compulsory education for children aged 6 to 14 years.
