About Dr. B.R. Ambedkar

Dr. Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar, a visionary leader and social reformer, is widely recognized as the principal architect of the Indian Constitution. His contributions to social justice, equality, and the right to education laid the foundation for a more inclusive and equitable society. Dr. Ambedkar, coming from a marginalized background, faced numerous challenges but became a powerful advocate for the rights of the oppressed and an inspirational figure in the movement for educational access and empowerment.
